# TRIM-HAA Risk Assessment

Status: draft for ethics review. This document does not claim approval.

| Risk | Likelihood | Severity | Mitigation | Residual Risk | Stopping Criterion |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Fatigue | Possible | Low | Short cases, breaks, stop option | Low | Participant reports excessive fatigue |
| Frustration | Possible | Low | Practice case, procedural help, skip where permitted | Low | Participant asks to stop |
| Perceived evaluation of intelligence | Possible | Low | State pilot tests workflow, not competence | Low | Participant shows distress |
| Pressure to agree with AI | Possible | Low to moderate | AI is not an answer key; disagreement acceptable | Low | Participant reports feeling pressured |
| Confusing AI output | Possible | Low | Frozen AI shown with cautionary instructions | Low | Repeated confusion blocks task |
| Free-text privacy | Possible | Moderate | Pseudonymous IDs, instruction not to include personal details, review before sharing | Low to moderate | Personal details appear repeatedly |
| Accidental identity disclosure | Unlikely | Moderate | Separate consent/contact storage; de-identification review | Low | Identifier appears in research dataset |
| Prior familiarity with source texts | Possible | Low | Background and contamination screening | Low | Participant has detailed prior case exposure |
| Researcher influence | Possible | Moderate | Non-leading researcher script | Low | Researcher gives interpretive guidance |
| Model-output bias | Possible | Low to moderate | AI not answer key; participant may reject | Low | Output is identified as harmful or inappropriate |
| Copyrighted text | Possible | Moderate | Case-selection legal review | Low after review | Case lacks legal basis for use |
| Data breach | Unlikely | Moderate | Encrypted storage, access control, breach process | Low | Suspected unauthorized access |

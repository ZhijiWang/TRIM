# TRIM-HAA ethics preparation

Do not claim ethics approval until it is granted or an exemption is documented.

Checklist:

- [ ] Human-research ethics review or exemption.
- [ ] Informed consent.
- [ ] Disclosure that AI assistance may be shown.
- [ ] Explanation of revision logging.
- [ ] Explanation of confidence, uncertainty, trust, or ownership questions if used.
- [ ] Anonymised participant IDs.
- [ ] Free-text rationale privacy review.
- [ ] Source-text copyright review.
- [ ] Data retention schedule.
- [ ] Withdrawal procedure.
- [ ] Compensation plan.
- [ ] Public/raw annotation publication policy.
- [ ] Model-provider data-handling plan.
- [ ] Reuse review for historical coder submissions.
- [ ] Contamination screening for prior access to AI/human annotations.

Historical coder submissions must not be converted automatically into human-AI records. Reuse requires consent, contamination, and provenance review.

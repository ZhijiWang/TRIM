# TRIM-HAA minimum pilot protocol

Status: instrumentation and feasibility pilot.

This pilot must not claim an exposure effect.

## Purpose

- test whether participants can complete TRIM-HAA Core;
- test human-pre lock and post-AI revision flow;
- test provenance completeness;
- estimate completion time;
- identify copying, fatigue, and field confusion.

## Design

- 4-6 participants;
- 4-6 cases;
- one frozen AI model output per case;
- TRIM-HAA Core only;
- locked human-pre record;
- AI exposure;
- human-post revision;
- at least one no-AI second-pass control assignment;
- counterbalanced case assignment;
- short post-case revision-reason question;
- completion-time logging;
- burden and comprehension feedback.

## Revision-reason options

- AI identified evidence I had missed.
- AI clarified a category boundary.
- AI confirmed my original judgment.
- AI persuaded me to change my interpretation.
- I changed after rereading rather than because of AI.
- I rejected the AI output.
- Other.

## Minimum outputs

- completion rate;
- missing fields;
- time per case;
- validation issues;
- label adoption;
- evidence adoption;
- mechanism adoption;
- uncertainty shift;
- alternative suppression/generation;
- copied-phrase warnings;
- participant burden notes.

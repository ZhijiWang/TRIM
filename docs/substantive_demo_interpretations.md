# Substantive Demonstration Interpretations

TRIM's comparison software locates structural relations among annotations. The interpretations below develop those relations into researcher-authored readings grounded in `data/demo_annotations.csv`.

The demonstrations move across three scales: within-text differentiation, shared loci with divergent trajectories, and cross-traditional threshold convergence. Together they show how annotation signatures become comparative arguments.

## Generated and Curated Layers

The generated same-function table identifies shared function labels alongside differing threshold-rationale signatures. Its `comparison_prompt` directs attention to the different evidence-to-function pathways.

The curated layer then brings four elements together:

- checked annotations;
- textual evidence;
- worked interpretation;
- the next empirical test.

## 1. Locus Migration within *Macbeth*

| Case | Function label | Friction locus | Rationale mechanism | Temporal orientation | Uncertainty |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `MAC_1_3` | `ambition_trigger_authorization` | `warrant_attribution` | `authorizes+reframes` | `prospective` | `medium` |
| `MAC_4_1` | `false_security` | `operation_function` | `reframes+narrows` | `prospective-retrospective` | `low` |
| `MAC_5_8` | `retrospective_trap` | `temporal_layering` | `reframes` | `retrospective` | `low` |

In Act 1.3, confirmation of Macbeth's new title gives the witches' speech practical authority. In Act 4.1, Macbeth converts equivocal claims into operational security by narrowing their meanings. In Act 5.8, Macduff's disclosure reorganizes the earlier assurance and reveals confidence as an effect of equivocation.

The same prophetic apparatus therefore moves through three loci:

> warrant acquisition → operational narrowing → retrospective reclassification

TRIM captures a change in the location of interpretive difficulty while the cue family remains stable.

**Pilot proposition:** independent coding tests whether the same three-stage sequence reappears and where any disagreement begins.

## 2. Shared Locus, Divergent Epistemic Trajectories

| Case | Source | Function label | Friction locus | Rationale mechanism | Temporal orientation | Uncertainty |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `ZZ_XI_4` | *Zuo zhuan* | `extended_deliberation` | `warrant_relation` | `extends` | `recursive` | `medium` |
| `GROVE_TAKEHIRO` | *In a Grove* | `epistemic_suspension` | `warrant_relation` | `contradicts+suspends` | `retrospective` | `high` |

In `ZZ_XI_4`, turtle-shell and milfoil results remain in conflict, and ranking speech adds another layer of authority. Their relation extends deliberation and keeps judgement active.

In `GROVE_TAKEHIRO`, posthumous testimony enters an already incompatible testimonial field. It adds another warrant, contradicts prior accounts, and suspends closure.

Both cases place friction in `warrant_relation`, yet their mechanisms separate:

- `ZZ_XI_4` extends judgement;
- `GROVE_TAKEHIRO` contradicts and suspends.

This comparison explains why `friction_locus` and `rationale_mechanism` form separate axes. The locus identifies the point under pressure; the mechanism records what that pressure does.

**Pilot proposition:** independent coding tests whether both cases retain `warrant_relation` while preserving their different mechanisms.

| Case | Source | Cue family | Function label | Friction locus | Rationale mechanism | Temporal orientation | Uncertainty |

| `ZZ_MIN_1` | *Zuo zhuan* | `divination` | `immediate_stabilization` | `warrant_attribution` | `stabilizes+projects` | `prospective` | `medium` |
| `MAC_1_3` | *Macbeth* | `prophecy` | `ambition_trigger_authorization` | `warrant_attribution` | `authorizes+reframes` | `prospective` | `medium` |

In `ZZ_MIN_1`, Xin Liao's interpretation gives the result stable prospective force and projects lineage significance. In `MAC_1_3`, partial confirmation gives the witches' words practical authority and reframes kingship as actionable.

The cases differ in tradition, genre, cue family, function, and consequence. They converge at `warrant_attribution` and prospective orientation while preserving distinct conversion pathways:

- `ZZ_MIN_1` stabilizes and projects;
- `MAC_1_3` authorizes and reframes.

TRIM therefore makes structural convergence visible while preserving cultural and functional specificity.

## 4. Same-Function Comparisons

- `ZZ_XIANG_7` — `operation_function / stabilizes / textual_anchor+ritual_sequence / intradiegetic / immediate / low`
- `ZZ_MIN_1` — `warrant_attribution / stabilizes+projects / textual_anchor+internal_sequence / intradiegetic / prospective / medium`

The shared function is produced through different authority structures. Xiang 7 restores ritual sequence and procedural intelligibility. Min 1 grants the result warranting force and projects that force into lineage significance.

### `extended_deliberation`

- `ZZ_XI_4` — `warrant_relation / extends / textual_anchor+internal_sequence / intradiegetic / recursive / medium`

Xi 4 extends deliberation through competing warrants. Zhuang 22 keeps the sign available across projection, descent, and later historical readability. One pathway extends horizontally through conflict; the other extends vertically through temporal uptake.

## Comparative Payoff

The demonstrations build a cumulative case:

> within-text differentiation → shared locus with divergent trajectories → cross-traditional threshold convergence

This progression shows what the signature contributes beyond cue and function labels. Evidence, anchor, locus, mechanism, support, discourse position, temporality, uncertainty, and alternatives become parts of one comparative object.

The blinded pilot turns these readings into three pre-specified tests. The software locates the structural relations; the researcher develops their substantive meaning and returns every claim to the underlying text.

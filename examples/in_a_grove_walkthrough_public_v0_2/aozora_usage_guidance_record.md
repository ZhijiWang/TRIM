## Relevant official guidance

The official Aozora Bunko handling rules distinguish works whose copyright has expired from works still under copyright. For expired-copyright works, the rules permit downloading, copying, redistribution, sharing, format conversion, and adaptation without payment or prior permission from Aozora Bunko.

The rules also request preservation of provenance information, including the work title, author, base edition, input and proofreading history, file dates, and notes describing the Aozora Bunko file's origin. When wording, orthography, format, or annotations are changed, the rules expect the work history and changes to be documented.

The same guidance warns that translations can carry separate translator copyright even when the original author's copyright has expired. This is why the public walkthrough uses Akutagawa's Japanese text as canonical evidence and does not redistribute the Takashi Kojima translation.

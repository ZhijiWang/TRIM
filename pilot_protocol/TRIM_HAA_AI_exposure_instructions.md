# TRIM-HAA AI Exposure Instructions

In some second-pass cases, you will see a frozen AI-generated annotation.

The AI output is a generated annotation, not an answer key. It may be correct, incomplete, misleading, or differently reasoned.

You may accept it, partially accept it, or reject it. Submit the judgment you are willing to defend. Do not assume fluent wording means correctness. Do not copy text unless it genuinely represents your submitted justification.

Disagreement is acceptable. Uncertainty may increase or decrease. Alternatives may be retained, added, changed, or removed.

## AI Components Shown

The displayed AI record may include:

- Primary evidence
- Function label
- Rationale mechanism
- Uncertainty
- Rationale note
- Alternative pathway status and note where present

The displayed AI record must be frozen before participant data collection. No live model generation or model retry occurs during the participant session.

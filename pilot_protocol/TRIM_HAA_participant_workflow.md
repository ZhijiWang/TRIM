# TRIM-HAA Participant Workflow

Expected session length: approximately 60-90 minutes, including instructions, practice, breaks, annotation tasks, and feedback questions. This duration range is provisional and must be finalised before ethics submission and recruitment.

## Sequence

1. Participant reads the information sheet.
2. Participant provides consent.
3. Participant receives a pseudonymous ID.
4. Participant completes the background questionnaire.
5. Participant reads the Core instructions, label guide, and mechanism guide.
6. Participant completes a practice case.
7. Participant asks procedural questions only.
8. Participant completes assigned human-pre records while seeing the original segmented text and no AI output.
9. Each human-pre record is locked.
10. Participant proceeds to the assigned second-pass condition.
11. AI-review cases show the original text, the participant's own locked earlier response, one frozen AI-generated response, and an editable current-response form.
12. Control cases show the original text, the participant's own locked earlier response, no AI-generated material, and an editable current-response form.
13. Participant submits a revised or unchanged current response.
14. Participant selects a revision-reason option.
15. Participant completes burden and comprehension questions.
16. Participant is debriefed.

## Participant Assurances

- The study does not collect hidden chain-of-thought.
- Participants are not asked to reveal private internal reasoning.
- `rationale_note` is a short submitted justification.
- Participants may reject AI output.
- Agreement with AI is not rewarded.
- Unchanged answers are acceptable.
- Uncertainty is acceptable.
- Alternative interpretations are acceptable.
- The task evaluates the workflow, not participant ability.

# TRIM-HAA Feasibility Analysis Plan

This plan is descriptive. It contains no powered hypothesis test.

## Primary Feasibility Outputs

- Completion rate
- Validation success
- Lock-verification success
- Exposure-link completeness
- Missingness by field
- Procedural question count
- Participant-reported confusion
- Burden responses
- Completion and review times if collected
- Frequency of unchanged records
- Frequency of post-AI revisions
- Frequency of control revisions
- Warning counts

## Secondary Descriptive Outputs

- Label adoption
- AI evidence incorporation
- Increased evidence convergence
- Evidential displacement
- Mechanism adoption
- Uncertainty shift
- Alternative suppression, generation, and modification
- Rationale overlap

## Limits

The pilot will not make:

- Causal claims
- Powered hypothesis-test claims
- No accuracy claim
- Gold-standard claims
- Claims that AI agreement equals quality
- Claims that participant self-report proves influence

Results will be used to decide whether the instrument, workflow, and ethics materials are ready for a larger study.

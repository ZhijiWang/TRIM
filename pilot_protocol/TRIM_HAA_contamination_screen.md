# TRIM-HAA Contamination Screen

Use this screen before formal pilot participation. Do not automatically reuse historical coder submissions.

## Screening Questions

Have you previously seen any of the following?

- The AI outputs for this pilot
- Earlier coder records for these cases
- The study manuscript
- Adjudicated cases
- Detailed case analyses
- The exact study hypothesis
- Previous versions of this pilot

## Classification

Eligible:

- No prior exposure to pilot materials or exact hypothesis.

Eligible with disclosure:

- General familiarity with the topic but no access to AI outputs, records, adjudications, or exact cases.

Exclude from formal pilot:

- Prior exposure to AI outputs, adjudicated cases, earlier coder records for the same cases, or exact pilot hypothesis.

Retain only as usability tester:

- Prior involvement in developing the instrument, manuscript, or pilot materials.

Record the classification and reason using the pseudonymous participant ID only.

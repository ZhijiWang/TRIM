# TRIM-HAA Researcher Session Script

## Opening

Thank you for considering participation. Today you will complete a small feasibility task for a text-annotation workflow. This study evaluates the workflow, not your intelligence, literary ability, or ability to outperform AI.

Expected session length: approximately 60-90 minutes, including instructions, practice, breaks, annotation tasks, and feedback questions. This duration range is provisional and must be finalised before ethics submission and recruitment.

The AI-generated annotations, when shown, are research stimuli and are not answer keys.

## Researcher May Explain

- Written definitions
- The approved synthetic practice example
- Standardised procedural instructions
- Interface operation
- How to save or submit a record
- How the practice case works

## Researcher May Not

- Suggest a label
- Suggest evidence
- Interpret the case
- Evaluate the participant
- Say the AI answer is correct
- Resolve category decisions
- Pressure participants to revise
- Create new case-specific examples
- Say whether a mechanism applies to the current case
- Compare participant quality with AI quality
- Praise agreement with AI
- Express surprise at disagreement

## Standard Responses

Question: "Which label should I choose?"

Response: "Please choose the label you think best fits, using the written instructions. I cannot advise on the interpretation."

Question: "What does this mechanism mean in this case?"

Response: "I can repeat the written definition and the approved practice example, but I cannot tell you whether it applies to this case."

Question: "Is the AI answer right?"

Response: "The AI-generated response is not an answer key. Please submit the response that best represents your current judgment."

Question: "Can I leave my answer unchanged?"

Response: "Yes. Unchanged answers are acceptable."

Question: "Should I be uncertain?"

Response: "Use the uncertainty level that best represents your submitted annotation. Uncertainty is acceptable."

Question: "Can I stop?"

Response: "Yes. You may stop now. We will follow the withdrawal procedure described in the information sheet."

## Required Session Log

Log:

- Procedural questions
- Non-standard explanations
- Technical interruptions
- Protocol deviations
- Researcher intervention time

Do not record private participant reasoning beyond the approved research fields.

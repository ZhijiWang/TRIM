# TRIM-HAA Data Management Plan

Status: draft for ethics review. This document does not claim approval.

## Participant IDs

Research records use pseudonymous participant IDs. Contact and consent records are stored separately from annotation data.

## Data Categories

- Core annotation records
- Assistance provenance records
- Exposure-event records
- Lock-manifest records
- Model-output records
- Prompt and model manifests
- Background questionnaire responses
- Revision-reason responses
- Burden and comprehension responses
- Protocol deviations
- Timing fields if collected

## Free Text

Rationale notes and optional comments may contain sensitive or identifying information. Participants are instructed not to include personal information. Researchers will review free text before quotation or sharing.

Hidden chain-of-thought is never collected.

## Storage and Security

Data will be stored in encrypted institutional or approved storage. Access is limited to approved researchers. Backups follow institutional policy.

## Model-Provider Handling

No live model calls occur during participant sessions. Frozen AI records are prepared before data collection. No participant data is sent to a model provider during the session.

## Publication and Sharing

Aggregate results may be published. De-identified rationale excerpts may be quoted only with optional consent. Raw-data sharing will exclude direct identifiers and be reviewed for accidental disclosure.

## Repository Separation

Pilot research data should be stored separately from public code. No real participant data should be committed to the repository.

# TRIM-HAA Feasibility Success Criteria

These criteria are provisional and require pilot-team and ethics-review approval where thresholds are involved.

## Provisional Success Criteria

- All required records validate, or deviations are explainable and logged.
- All human-pre locks verify.
- All AI exposures link correctly.
- Participants can complete the Core after practice.
- No Core field shows near-universal misunderstanding.
- Missingness is low enough to support a larger study.
- Session burden is acceptable.
- No serious privacy or technical incident occurs.
- Reports can be generated without manual reconstruction.
- At least some observations distinguish incorporation, convergence, and displacement.
- Control second-pass change is observable separately.

## Thresholds Not Yet Fixed

Precise numerical thresholds for missingness, burden, incomplete records, and procedural-question counts must be justified and approved before recruitment.

# TRIM-HAA Case Selection Criteria

Cases are selected to test the instrument, not to estimate population prevalence.

## Criteria

Cases should:

- Be short enough for repeated reading.
- Contain more than one plausible interpretive route.
- Have clearly segmented source text.
- Permit a project-defined function label.
- Avoid highly distressing content.
- Avoid unnecessary cultural or linguistic barriers.
- Not require extensive external knowledge.
- Support at least one plausible AI-human disagreement.
- Be legally usable for the intended research and sharing plan.

## Review Before Use

Substantive cases are not finalized by this package. They must be separately reviewed for copyright, participant burden, cultural or linguistic barriers, and distressing content before ethics submission.

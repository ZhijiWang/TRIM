# Future Human-Subject Study Drafts

Status: Deferred future human-subject study.

No ethics approval has been obtained. These materials are not executable for recruitment, consent, data collection, or participant contact without institutional adaptation and approval.

The files in `ethics_drafts/` and `pilot_protocol/` are future ethics-submission drafts. They preserve the planned study language, workflow, and data dictionary for later review.

Empirical human validation is reserved for a future approved study, potentially during doctoral research. The current repository does not claim human validation, causal effects of model exposure, participant review, or ethics readiness.

Before any use with participants, the materials require:

- institution-specific ethics review;
- legal and copyright review of any source packets;
- external plain-language review;
- final case and vocabulary selection;
- confirmed retention periods and institutional contacts;
- approved recruitment, consent, withdrawal, and data-management language.
